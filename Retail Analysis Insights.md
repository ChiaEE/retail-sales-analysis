# **Retail Sales Analysis Insights**

## **Executive Summary**

This analysis examines retail transaction data to identify revenue trends, customer purchasing behavior, and product performance. The results highlight which product categories generate the most revenue, which customer demographics drive the most sales, and how revenue changes across months.

# **1\. Revenue Overview**

### **Total Revenue**

Query: Total revenue from all transactions.

SELECT SUM(Total\_Amount) FROM raw\_retail\_sales;

Insight:

* The dataset generated **$456000 in total revenue** across all recorded transactions.

Interpretation:  
This represents the overall financial performance captured in the dataset.

### **Average Transaction Value**

Query: Average transaction value across purchases.

SELECT AVG(Price\_Per\_Unit) FROM raw\_retail\_sales;

Insight:

* The **average transaction value was $179.89**.

Interpretation: This indicates the typical amount spent during a single purchase.

### **Largest Single Transaction**

Query: Maximum value of a single transaction.

Insight:

* The **largest single transaction recorded was $2000**.

Interpretation: This represents the highest-value purchase in the dataset.

### **Smallest Single Transaction**

Query: Minimum value of a transaction.

Insight:

* The **smallest recorded transaction was $25**.

Interpretation: This likely reflects purchases involving a small quantity or lower-priced items.

# **2\. Product Category Performance**

### **Revenue by Product Category**

Query: Total revenue generated by each product category.

SELECT Product\_Category, SUM(Total\_Amount) AS Revenue

FROM raw\_retail\_sales

GROUP BY Product\_Category;

Insight:

| Product Category | Revenue |
| :---- | :---- |
| Beauty | $143,515 |
| Clothing | $155,580 |
| Electronics | $156,905 |

* The **highest revenue-generating category was Electronics with $155,905 in sales**.

* Other categories generated between **$143,515 and $155,580 in revenue**.

### **Total Quantity Sold per Category**

Query: Total number of units sold by category.

SELECT Product\_Category, SUM(Quantity) AS Total\_Quantity

FROM raw\_retail\_sales

GROUP BY Product\_Category 

ORDER BY Product\_Category DESC; 

Insight:

* The category with the **largest number of units sold was Clothing**.

* This category sold **894 total units**.

| Product Category | Total Quantity |
| :---- | :---- |
| Beauty | 849 |
| Clothing | 894 |
| Electronics | 771 |

Interpretation:  
 High unit sales may indicate strong customer demand or lower price points.

### **Category With Most Units Sold**

Query: Ranking categories by total units sold.

SELECT Product\_Category, SUM(Price\_Per\_Unit) AS Total\_Units

FROM raw\_retail\_sales

GROUP BY Product\_Category 

ORDER BY Total\_Units DESC; 

Insight:

* The category with the **highest total unit sales was Electronics**.

| Product Category | Total Units |
| :---- | :---- |
| Electronics | $62,210 |
| Clothing | $61,175 |
| Beauty | $56,505 |

Interpretation: This product group appears to be the most frequently purchased.

# **3\. Customer Demographics**

### **Average Customer Age**

Query: Average age of customers in the dataset.

SELECT AVG(Age) FROM raw\_retail\_sales;

Insight:

* The **average customer age is 41 years old**.

Interpretation: This provides a general profile of the customer base.

### **Transactions by Gender**

Query: Number of transactions made by each gender.

SELECT Gender, COUNT(Transaction\_ID) AS Transaction\_Count

FROM raw\_retail\_sales

GROUP BY Gender

ORDER BY Transaction\_Count;

Insight:

* **Female customers made the most transactions with 510 purchases**.

Interpretation: This suggests that this demographic group may be the most active customer segment.

### **Revenue by Gender**

Query: Total revenue generated by each gender group.

SELECT Gender, SUM(Total\_Amount) AS Revenue

FROM raw\_retail\_sales

GROUP BY Gender

ORDER BY Revenue DESC; 

Insight:

* **Female customers generated the most revenue, totaling $232,840**.

Interpretation: This indicates that this demographic contributes significantly to total sales.

### **Revenue by Age**

Query: Revenue generated by customers of each age.

SELECT Age, SUM(Total\_Amount) AS Revenue

FROM raw\_retail\_sales

GROUP BY Age

ORDER BY Revenue DESC;

Insight:

* Customers aged **43 generated the highest revenue of $17,970**.

Interpretation: Certain age groups may be more likely to spend more per transaction.

# **4\. Time-Based Sales Trends**

### **Revenue by Month**

Query: Monthly revenue totals.

SELECT MONTH(Transaction\_Date) AS Month\_Number, SUM(Total\_Amount) AS Revenue

FROM raw\_retail\_sales

GROUP BY Month\_Number

ORDER BY Month\_Number ASC;

Insight:

* Revenue fluctuates across the year, with monthly totals ranging from **$36,980 to $46,580**.

Interpretation: This suggests that purchasing activity varies across different months.

### **Number of Transactions by Month**

Query: Number of transactions per month.

SELECT MONTH(Transaction\_Date) AS Month\_Number, COUNT(Transaction\_ID) AS Transactions

FROM raw\_retail\_sales

GROUP BY Month\_Number

ORDER BY Month\_Number ASC;

Insight:

* The **highest number of transactions occurred in May**.

Interpretation: This month likely experienced increased customer activity.

### **Month With Highest Revenue**

Query: Ranking months by revenue.

SELECT MONTH(Transaction\_Date) AS Month\_Number, SUM(Total\_Amount) AS Revenue

FROM raw\_retail\_sales

GROUP BY Month\_Number

ORDER BY Revenue DESC;

Insight:

* The **highest revenue month was May with $53,150 in sales**.

Interpretation: This could indicate seasonal demand or promotional activity during this time.
